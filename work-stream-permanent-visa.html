Secure your Work Permanent Resident Visa

The Australia Permanent Resident Visa is your ticket to fast-track permanent residency in one of the world’s most welcoming countries.

Explore visa options for working in Australia

If you want to spend time working in Australia you will need a work visa, the type of visa you may get will depend on how long you want to stay, it is likely that you will need to have an occupation that is on a skills shortage list.

Subclass 489

Skilled Regional (Provisional) visa

For skilled workers who want to live and work in regional Australia
You can bring your family
If you are granted this visa, you might be able to apply for the Skilled Regional (Permanent) visa (subclass 887)

Stay

​​​​​​​Up to 4 years

Cost

AUD4,240

Processing Time

​Processing times vary

  • You must be nominated by a state or territory government
  • You must live in a regional or low-population area
  • You must have a points score above 65

Subclass 190

Skilled Nominated visa

This visa lets nominated skilled workers live and work in Australia as permanent residents.


Stay

Permanently

Cost

From AUD4,640.00

Processing Time

For an indication of processing times for this visa, use the visa processing time guide tool. This will show the processing times for recently decided applications. It is a guide only and not specific to your application.
  • ​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​You must be nominated by an Australian state or territory government agency
  • You must score at least 65 points on our points test to be eligible
  • You must be aged under 45 to apply

Subclass 189

Skilled Independent visa

This visa is for invited workers, eligible New Zealand citizens and eligible Hong Kong or British National (Overseas) passport holders with skills we need, to live and work permanently anywhere in Australia.

Stay

Permanently

Cost

From AUD4,115

Processing Time

See the relevant stream
  • ​You must be a skilled worker, or
  • You must be a New Zealand citizen holding a Special category visa (subclass 444) who has been living in Australia for the last 5 years

Subclass 132

Business Talent (Permanent) visa

This visa allows you to establish a new or develop an existing business in Australia.

Stay

​​Permanent

Cost

From AUD7,855

Processing Time

Check the relevant stream for processing times

  • You must be nominated by a state or territory government agency
  • You must be invited to apply for the visa
  • You must have funding or assets greater than AUD1 million




Subclass 482

Temporary Skill Shortage visa

This temporary visa lets an employer sponsor a suitably skilled worker to fill a position they can’t find a suitably skilled Australian to fill.

Stay

​​​​For up to 4 years

Cost

From AUD1,290

Processing Time

​Processing times vary​

  • This is a temporary visa
  • You must have an approved sponsor and be nominated for the visa
  • You must meet our English language requirements
