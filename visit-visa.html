Visitor Visa

Get Visitors Visa

A visitor visa is essentially a temporary permit that enables individuals to stay in a foreign country for a specific period without seeking permanent residency. It's designed for various purposes:

  • Short-term Business Activities: This includes attending conferences, meetings, or engaging in business-related tasks that don't involve establishing permanent employment in the host country.
  • Visiting Family/Friends: Individuals can use a visitor visa to spend time with their relatives or friends living in another country. This could involve celebrations, family reunions, or simply spending leisure time together.
  • Tourism: People often use visitor visas for sightseeing, exploring attractions, and experiencing the culture and traditions of a foreign country.
  • Medical Treatment: Visitor visas may also cover traveling abroad for medical purposes, enabling individuals to seek specialized healthcare or treatments not available in their home country.
